About me

I am a chilean astrophysicist and data analyst based in Munich. I earned my Ph.D. in Astrophysics from the Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität. I have worked exploring galaxies and the distant universe, using different computational tools to understand how cosmic structures evolve. Along the way, I discovered that I really enjoy the process itself: analysing data, building models, uncovering patterns, and translating complexity into clarity. Also, communicating those discoveries to a general public is something that really inspires me. I believe science has the power to connect people, and I’m excited about contributing to that through analysis, creativity, and communication.

My Research

My research has been mainly focused on studying the gaseous reservoirs in and around high-redshift galaxies.
